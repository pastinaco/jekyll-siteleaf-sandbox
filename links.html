Indivisible Guide

Indivisible San Jose is a local chapter of the Indivisible movement, based on indivisibleguide.com. The guide is an excellent introduction to effectively resisting the Trump agenda.

Flippable

Indivisible San Jose working actively with flippable.org to support the following special elections:

  • Jon Ossoff in the upcoming June 20th US House GA-6 runoff
  • Rob Quist in the May 25th election for Montana’s US House election
  • Michela Skelton in the August 8 election for the Missouri State House MO-50 election

We will be working on other races to be determined as well, including Flippable’s “Flip VA Blue” efforts. If you’d like to help please attend the next Indivisible San Jose meeting, or check in on our Facebook group, or sign up on the Flippable web site.

Sister District

The Sister District Project organizes residents of relatively “safe blue” districts like CA-18 and CA-19 (where most of Indivisible San Jose members are) to volunteer in a “winnable red” district where their energy and involvement can make a difference. Indivisible San Jose is working with Sister District to provide organizing help for our sister districts in the 2018 midterms, and possibly some special elections before then.

Swing Left

Swing Left is similar to Sister Districts but is focusing on a specific set of Swing Districts. For Indivisible San Jose there is a nearby Swing District in CA-10.
